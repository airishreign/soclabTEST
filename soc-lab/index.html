About the SOC Lab

Watch our story unfold - from vision to a world-class cybersecurity learning environment

Our Vision

The SOC Lab at De La Salle Lipa represents a commitment to developing the next generation of cybersecurity professionals. Through hands-on training, industry partnerships, and real-world experience, we're creating a pipeline of skilled professionals ready to defend against evolving cyber threats.

Our program combines academic excellence with practical application, ensuring our students are industry-ready from day one.

The Journey Begins

From concept to reality - building a world-class cybersecurity learning environment

Journey Timeline

The Birth

ICTC RISE Framework Initiative

Started as part of ICTC RISE Framework to take OJT to the next level. Conceptualized the SOC Lab, later termed as Cybersecurity Lab. Launched Google Forms for interested students wanting to be ICTC OJT or Mentee.

February 5, 2025

Concept & Design Phase

GSD released the renovation plan for MB 210. This marked the beginning of transforming vision into reality.

February 11, 2025

The Blueprint

TOIC drafted Cybersecurity Learning Lab Plan. This became the "bible" for the OJTs and Mentees, establishing the foundation for our structured approach.

February 26, 2025

Top Admin Approval

Initial setup of 5 temporary computers in the SOC Lab space, focusing on cost-effective solutions. Presentation and approval from Top Administration secured the future of the lab.

The SOC Lab at DLSL

A specialized Security Operations Center designed to provide hands-on experience, training, and research opportunities in cybersecurity

Mission

A platform for students, faculty, and IT staff to simulate, analyze, and defend against cyber threats while developing critical skills in cybersecurity.

Location

MB 210 - A state-of-the-art facility equipped with modern technology and monitoring platforms conducive to learning.

Partnership

Learning Labs initiative in collaboration with DysrupIT Australia, CITE, and ICTC, bringing international expertise to DLSL.

Infrastructure

Equipped with new hardware, monitoring platforms, and tools essential for comprehensive cybersecurity training and operations.

First Deployment Milestone

February 10, 2025 - Marked the initial deployment of ICTC OJT under a developed, formal, and structured OJT Program with 2 BS Computer Science students from the College of Information Technology and Engineering.

Program Highlights:

  • All applicants underwent stages of interview and orientation prior to deployment
  • Deployed OJT uses Timestation for attendance tracking
  • Participated in WorldMun Socio-Venture Challenge on March 17, 2025
  • Spearheaded the development of Turnstile App integrated to SMS
  • Coached by industry experts
